## FAQ

### Is this a procurement platform?

No. ModelMeter is a lightweight audit and reporting tool. It helps identify obvious AI spend issues before renewal or budget review.

### Do I need to create an account?

No. You can run the audit without logging in. Email capture happens only after the report is visible.

### Are the savings guaranteed?

No. Savings are modeled estimates based on submitted inputs and public plan benchmarks. The report shows assumptions so a billing owner can verify them.

### Why does Credex appear on high-savings reports?

Credex helps teams access discounted AI infrastructure credits. If the audit shows significant savings potential, Credex may be relevant to the next step.

### What happens to my email?

Email is stored separately from the public report. Public report URLs show tools and savings, not contact details.
